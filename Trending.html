JIM CORBETT NATIONAL PARK

Distance from Delhi :250 kms


The Corbett National Park is a protected area for the mighty yet endangered Bengal tigers of India.Besides, it is home to varied species of about 50 trees and animals, 580 birds and 25 reptiles.

Known as tiger country, Corbett is where you should head to observe wildlife in its natural habitat. The perfect terrain with a river and numerous tributaries flowing through it, Corbett promises an adventure that youâ€™ll never forget. For cheap holiday package... opt for a government run property within the national park or a budget hotel outside the park boundary. Corbett holiday packages offer a plethora of options, just take your pick and enjoy your Corbett trip. Jungle safaris into the national park are conducted every morning and afternoon, accompanied by a professional guide or naturalist. On a Corbett tour, you can opt for a jeep safari or an elephant safari, as per your preference. In the morning, the park remains open from 6 am to 11 am while afternoon safari timings are from 2:30 pm to 5:30 pm. Besides a jungle safari, you can go for a stroll along the river or enjoy fishing and nature walks around Corbett. The main areas in Corbett National Park include Dhikala, Sarapduli, Bijrani and Ghairal besides Ramnagar, which although outside the park, offers the maximum accommodation options. One of the oldest wildlife reserves in the country, Corbett National Park is also part of Project Tiger, a wildlife protection initiative that concentrates mainly on protecting and conserving tiger population in the country.

Four Zones-

Jim Corbett National Park is sprawled in a vast area and is divided into four different zones. These are the Durga Dev Safari Zone, the Dhikala Zone, the Bijrani Safari Zone and the Zhirna Safari Zone. The chances of spotting a tiger are high in the Dhikala Zone. This is the perfect place to see the endangered Royal Bengal Tiger in all its majesty. Sitabani- Sitabani is the perfect place for avid bird watchers. Unique species of fauna can be spotted here. With a suitable Jim Corbett package, these natural attractions can be enjoyed.


Weather

You can plan your visit anytime of the year but November to June is the perfect time to visit this destination. Jim Corbett packages are available round the year.
